Who is Vetted

Vetted is an audio, video, and text communication platform that links veterans with shared experiences and interests in an online environment with consistency, compassion, and connection as a priority. Through identity verification and complete anonymity we are able to provide veterans with a protected place to communicate whenever they want, wherever they want, how they want.
